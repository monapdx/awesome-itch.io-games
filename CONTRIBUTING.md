## General Criteria

1. Strong preference for games that can be **played in the browser**
2. Most games featured here skew towards **casual** play
3. Bonus points (+) are given to games with **high replay value**
4. My own personal favorite genres are overrepresented here and include: narrative & story-driven, word games, puzzle, arcade, simulation, cooking games, time management, city builders, sandbox & experimental games, retro pixel aesthetics, hidden object, trivia, card games, etc)
5. This list is meant to surface games that are a bit **unpolished** or in **early development**. I like underground, weird **indie games**!
6. All of the games listed here are **free to play** and do not require payment (although a few are demos)
