## General Criteria

- [ ] Strong preference for games that can be **played in the browser**
- [ ] Most games featured here skew towards **casual** play
- [ ] Bonus points (+) are given to games with **high replay value**
- [ ] My own personal favorite genres are overrepresented here and include: narrative & story-driven, word games, puzzle, arcade, simulation, cooking games, time management, city builders, sandbox & experimental games, retro pixel aesthetics, hidden object, trivia, card games, etc)
- [ ] This list is meant to surface games that are a bit **unpolished** or in **early development**. I like underground, weird **indie games**!
- [ ] All of the games listed here are **free to play** and do not require payment (although a few are demos)

- [ ] 🎮 If you've found an awesome game on **Itch.io**, you can 👉[submit it here](https://github.com/monapdx/awesome-itch.io-games/issues/new?template=submit-game.yml).
